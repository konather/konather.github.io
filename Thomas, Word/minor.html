Private Lesson/Exam Information

Audition: Minimum performance level will be the student's ability to obtain membership in a performing brass or percussion ensemble
Lessons: Four semesters of performance lessons are required One 25-minute lesson per week

Ensemble Requirements:


    Four semesters of participation is required in one of the following:

  1. Concert Band

  2. Symphony Orchestra

  3. Wind Band

  4. Trumpet Choir

  5. Horn Choir

  6. Trombone Choir

  7. Tuba-Euphonium Choir

  8. Brass Choir

Lesson Requirements:

Practice Requirements: 5-7 hours as recommended by teacher

Lesson Materials:

Students must provide their own method books, sticks and mallets

Recital attendance:

Attend at least two music recitals each semester

    Exam Requirements:

  1. A jury exam will be taken at the end of each semester of study


  2. One solo with piano accompaniment. Memory is optional.


  3. Technique as assigned by teacher


Recital Requirements:
A short 10-15 minute recital in a Brass or Percussion Performance Class will be encouraged, upon the teacher's recommendation, near the end of the student's private study
