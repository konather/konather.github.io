faculty bios

Completed his D.M.A. in trumpet performance and pedagogy in 1995 at the University of Southern Mississippi after earning a bachelor's degree from Bob Jones University and a master's degree from the University of Illinois. Bruce has performed with the Meridian Symphony Orchestra, Greenville Symphony Orchestra, and the Southern Arts Brass Quintet and currently plays with the Spartanburg Philharmonic Orchestra and the Foundation Brass. He is head of the Department of Instrumental Studies, director of the University Trumpet Choir and Concert Band and minister of music at Suber Road Baptist Church.
