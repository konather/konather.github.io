Non-majors

Private Lesson/Exam Information


Lessons: One 25-minute lesson per week

Practice Requirements: 3-5 hours as recommended by teacher.

Lesson Materials: Students must provide their own method books, sticks and mallets.

Recital Attendance: Attend at least two music recitals each semester.

Exam Requirements: A jury exam will be taken at the end of each semester of study. One solo with piano accompaniment. Memory is optional. Technique as assigned by teacher.
